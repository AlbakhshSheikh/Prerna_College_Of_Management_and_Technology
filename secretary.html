Secretary's Desk


"Prerna Sewa Mandal through its educational institutions wishes to reach all the strata of society , mainly the masses , with a view to providing them quality education in professional and technical fields and to enable them to contribute to the growth , development and progress of the nation and also to be a part of the changing global environment in every branch of life

To meet its aspirations Prerna Sewa Mandal runs Dr S.C.Gulhane Prerna College of Commerce, Science and Arts which runs 10 UG and 05 PG courses in Commerce , Science and Arts . Now by opening a professional and technical education institution named Dr S C Gulhane Prerna College of Management and Technology, Vihirgaon, Nagpur , Prerna Sewa Mandal has embarked on the historic journey of providing management and technical education to the students and convert them into valuable assets who would be able to apply their skills and knowledge to the nation building as well as be a part of the global level experiments in their respective areas . We are firmly committed to providing best quality of infrastructural facilities, most advance technology and well trained and well experienced faculty for the college to achieve our goals . We will also encourage , besides all necessary curricular activities , a number of co- curricular and extracurricular activities to add new dimensions to the skill , knowledge , training and experience of the students.

I wish all the best to the learners and the faculty as well and expect them to do their best to translate their dreams into reality ."
