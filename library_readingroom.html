LIBRARY AND READING ROOM

The library and reading room within a college campus stand as a cornerstone of academic exploration and intellectual growth. These spaces are meticulously designed to foster an environment conducive to learning, research, and the exchange of ideas. With shelves lined with an extensive collection of books, journals, and digital resources, the college library serves not just as a repository of knowledge, but as a gateway to the vast world of learning and discovery. The careful curation of materials ensures that students and faculty have access to the latest research in their fields, classic texts that have withstood the test of time, and a diverse array of perspectives that enrich the academic experience.

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Every student will be provided with the required number of books for the entire session.
  • The students will have to return the books before the final examination
  • The students at the time of receiving the books from the library should ensure that the book is not damaged in any manner. If the book is found damaged in any manner the student should immediately bring it to the notice of the librarian. Otherwise the responsibility of the damage book will entirely rest on the student.
  • If the issued book is lost, the students will have to return a new copy of the lost book. If he/she fails to do so, he/she will have to pay the amount charged by the librarian.
  • On-line journals available for students.
  • E-Books available for Students.

 
 

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Extra-Curricular books in Hindi, Marathi & English available for extra reading.
  • Reading Room in also available.
  • On demand soft copy of Syllabus, Question Papers, etc. provided to the stat faculty members by using email.
  • Library information such as New Arrivals, Library Rules, Journal List, Notices, News Paper Clipping, etc. are displayed on Library Notice Board from time to time.
  • Library is proud to provide online access facility to the students and faculty members to the internationally reputed commercial databases like DELNET, NDLI and so many (Paid or Free) databases.
  • Online Public Access Catalogue, which is a key to our entire collection of books, can also be accessed through library intranet.
  • Library orientation is given to the new students and new staff members of the college to allow them to use the library collection extensively to draw maximum benefits of the library facility.
