Welcome To Dr. S.C. Gulhane Prerna College of Management And Technology

Prerna Sewa Mandal, one of the pioneer organisations dedicated to the cause of education from its very inception, started its operations in the year 1974. This society has successfully completed 4 decades in the field of teaching and is recognised and appreciated for its steady development, achievements in the region. The formal body of the society which came into existence in 1983, aims for an all-round growth of the institution, as all are reputed academicians.

Currently it is functioning as a premier institution in the region dedicated to greater success and higher goals for students of commerce and science. It also runs a well-established school up to XII class with about 3,000 students. ‘Prerna Convent’ leaves no stone unturned to bring out the best in the pupils.

Prerna Junior College and Dr. S. C. Gulhane Prerna College of Commerce, Science & Arts are managed by highly qualified personalities and stalwart educationalists forming the Prerna Group under the Prerna Sewa Mandal.

word about Prerna Education and Research Academy, (a sister concern of Prerna Sewa Mandal); It was established to give more emphasis to research as it’s very name suggests. Research in every field has become a primary concern. Gulhane brothers who are well established and renowned in the educational scenario decided to expand and take a step further in the area of research.

Secretary of Prerna Sewa Mandal Dr. S. C. Gulhane who has always been a constant source of inspiration, tirelessly endeavours for the development of the college. He believes in not only educating the students but in making them self-reliant members of the society. His future vision is to bring up the Prerna Junior College and Prerna Senior College as one of the highly reputed Colleges in East and South Nagpur. The horizon may seem far but the sun does manage to reach there.



About College

Welcome to Dr S.C. Gulhane Prerna College Of Management and Technology , where you can access top-tier management education right in the heart of Nagpur! We are dedicated to molding the future leaders at Dr S.C. Gulhane Prerna College. Our goal is to provide a dynamic learning environment that promotes innovation, leadership, and professional growth. Situated in the bustling city of Nagpur, we take pride in delivering an educational experience that transforms our students and equips them with the skills, knowledge, and mindset needed to succeed in today's competitive business world. Here at Dr S.C. Gulhane Prerna College, we prioritize academic excellence and boast a faculty of industry experts and seasoned professionals.Our curriculum combines theory with hands-on practice to equip graduates with both knowledge and skills to confidently navigate real-world challenges in management. At our college, we know that the world of business is always changing. That's why we focus on being adaptable, innovative, and critical thinkers in our teaching. We believe in giving students hands-on learning experiences, working with businesses, and offering personalized guidance. Our goal is to help students be creative, make confident decisions, and lead with honesty.

Mission

"To provide necessary technical and professional education with a view to successfully preparing the students for their chosen career . To enable the students to contribute to the nation's progress from the global perspective. To create and provide a suitable platform to them where they can enhance their skill and knowledge level. To enable them to use their learnt skills and acquired knowledge for all practical purposes in a constructive manner . To encourage innovativd and research spirit among students to enable them to engage themselves in globally useful research To inculcate global ethics and human values in the students to make them globally compatible . Our overall mission is not to shape the future of the students but to create youths to shape the future of the world in a positive way by using their inquisitive , innovative and creative mindsets aiming the welfare of the whole of the humanity."



Vision

"Prerna Sewa Mandal remains firmly loyal to its commitment and its aim of providing sterling quality of value based, professional and technical education to the students and to empower them with the needed lifeskills , training , experience and enable them to face the global challenges in the changing global scenario and at the same time to transform them into world class responsible citizens."
